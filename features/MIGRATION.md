## Merge Legend

- **MERGE (dual-homed)** — ADR body in DECISIONS + design file with same ADR number. Combined into one FTR.
- **MERGE (same concept)** — ADR decision + DES specification about the same architectural concept. Rationale from ADR, Specification from DES.
- **MERGE (related)** — Multiple ADRs or DES sections covering facets of one concept. Combined with cross-references preserved.
- **Subsection** — DES identifier that lives inside a parent DES file. Becomes a subsection within the parent FTR; no standalone FTR number.
- **Absorbed** — Adopted PRO whose content was merged into an existing ADR/DES. No standalone FTR; noted in the target FTR's Notes section.
